Wasserstein gradient flows

The back-and-forth method [2] can be generalized to compute Wasserstein gradient flows (work in progress).

Videos

Here are a few examples of gradient flows of the entropy \begin{equation} F(\rho) = \gamma\int\rho(x)\ln\rho(x)\,dx + \int V(x)\,\rho(x)\,dx, \end{equation} where $V\colon\mathbb{R}^2\to\mathbb{R}\cup\{+\infty\}$ is a potential energy.

Here $V(x)=\iota_{S}(x)+\frac{1}{2}\lVert x-a \rVert^2$, where $S$ is the star domain and $a$ the lower right corner.
